Maurizio Cattelan

Maurizio Cattelan is a leading Italian contemporary artist known for his satirical and provocative sculptures. His works often challenge the boundaries between art and reality, sparking discussions on societal norms and values. One of his notable works includes "Frank and Jamie," a sculpture depicting two New York City policemen standing on their heads.

Anselm Kiefer

Anselm Kiefer is a renowned German painter and sculptor whose works delve into themes of history, memory, and mythology. His large-scale paintings are characterized by their textured surfaces and incorporation of materials like lead, ash, and straw. Kiefer's recent exhibitions, such as "Fallen Angels" at Palazzo Strozzi, explore profound biblical references and human experiences.

Maurizio Cattelan - "Comedian"

Maurizio Cattelan, an Italian conceptual artist, is best known for his satirical works.One of the most controversial and famous works of recent times, Comedian is a conceptual artwork featuring a banana duct-taped to a wall. Displayed at Art Basel Miami, it became a sensation for questioning the absurdity of the modern art market. The piece sold for $120,000 and later went viral when performance artist David Datuna ate the banana as part of his own act, Hungry Artist.

Anselm Kiefer - "The Morgenthau Plan"

Anselm Kiefer is known for his textured, monumental canvases that reflect Germany's history and trauma. The Morgenthau Plan is a powerful work referencing a post-WWII plan to deindustrialize Germany. The painting uses mixed media, including lead, ash, and straw, creating a somber, scorched-earth aesthetic that represents destruction and rebirth.

JR - "The Secret of the Great Pyramid"

French street artist JR is famous for his large-scale, illusionary installations. The Secret of the Great Pyramid was an enormous trompe-l'œil piece at the Louvre Pyramid in Paris, where he made it appear as if the glass pyramid was emerging from an underground excavation site. This temporary artwork drew thousands of spectators and highlighted the contrast between history and modernity.
